Directions from Airport to City Center

Please note that travel times vary heavily depending on the traffic!

Incheon International Airport




Things to do in the airport
  • Important! Buy a SIM card at the airport (Providers: KT, SK, LG).
  • Buy a "T-money" card at a convenience store and load 20,000–30,000 KRW (~20 euros). (Important! Must be paid in cash)
  • Take the all-stop train to the city (preferable to the express train).

Transportation
  • Transfer by Taxi: 50,000 KRW to the venue
  • By Train: Take Airport Railroad Express and transfer at Hongdae station to the venue
  • By Bus Take 6011 to the venue

  • More details: VisitSeoul, official Seoul, VisitKorea, Coex Center
