Vegetarian and Vegan in Seoul

While finding vegetarian or vegan options can require some effort, Korea has a long tradition of plant-based temple cuisine, and more recently, many stylish cafes and restaurants have emerged. We recommend the following resources to help your search:

Korea Herald Map: Maintained by the Korea Herald, an English-language newspaper in Korea.

Happy Cow: A global resource available as a website and app (iOS/Android).

Kakao Map (iOS/Android): Highly recommended for local navigation (often works better than Google Maps in Korea). You can find many user-curated (1) food (2) lists (3) there.
