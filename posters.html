Poster Guidelines

RECOMB 2025 Accepted Posters


Poster Specifications


Posters should be up to 84.1 cm wide by 118.9 cm tall (A0 paper format - 33.11 x 46.81 in) and printed in portrait mode. Please print your posters in advance.

Poster Presentation Schedule & Logistics


If you requested a change to your presentation day or presenting author and received a confirmation from us, your poster presentation will reflect the updated details as outlined in our email.

Even-numbered posters

  • Set-up: By the morning coffee break on April 26 (Day 1).
  • Presentation: After sessions conclude on April 26 (Day 1).
  • Take-down: By the lunch break on April 27 (Day 2).

Odd-numbered posters

  • Set-up: During the afternoon coffee break on April 27 (Day 2).
  • Presentation: After sessions conclude on April 27 (Day 2).
  • Take-down: By the end of sessions on April 28 (Day 3).

Note: Poster boards will be removed after sessions end on April 28 (Day 3). Any posters remaining on the boards at that time will be discarded.
